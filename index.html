About Us



We at InternCo are very passionate and dedicated to promoting the use of technology to protect the environment. We believe that technology has the potential to solve some of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time, and we are committed to developing and promoting innovative solutions.

We are inspired by the Tree of Technology, which symbolizes the intercorrectedness of all things. The roots of the tree represent foundational technolgies that support our modern world, such as energy, transportation, and communication. The branches of the tree represent the emerging technologies that have the potential to revolutionize our relationship with the environment, such as renewable enrgy, sustainable agriculture, and green chemistry.

We believe that by working together, we can harness the power of technology to create a more sustainable future for all.
